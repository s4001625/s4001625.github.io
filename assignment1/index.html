web review

 

1. What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?
When I arrived at the home page, the loading page caught my attention. After it finished, I focused on the elements on the home page, which is a bit crowded but still looks elegant. Also, the website's scrolling effect is interesting since it has a unique movement that not many other websites have.

2. Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.
1. Playing with the mouse arrow.
2. Trying to figure out which element or object is clickable.
3. Figuring out how to change the background colour for each project.
4. Clicking on video that is shown on the main page for the projects.

What part of the experience did you spend the most time engaging with?
The discipline section of the website because each discipline has its color. When we move our mouse near or on top of the picture, it can follow our mouse angle. For example, if our mouse is at the picture's bottom left corner, it will automatically tilt, looking like the bottom left corner is the center point.
