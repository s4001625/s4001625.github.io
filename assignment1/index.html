RICH BROWN

web review

1. What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

When I arrived at the home page, the loading page caught my attention. After it finished, I focused on the elements on the home page, which is a bit crowded but still looks elegant. Also, the website's scrolling effect is interesting since it has a unique movement that not many other websites have.

2. Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.

  • Playing with the line that follows the cursor.
  • Trying to figure out which object or element is interactive.
  • Figuring out how to change the background color from each disciplines.
  • Click and explore each video for Brown's previous projects.
  • scrolling up and down the website.

3. What part of the experience did you spend the most time engaging with?

The discipline section of the website because each project has its color. When we move our mouse near or on top of the picture, it can follow our mouse angle. For example, if our mouse is at the picture's bottom left corner, it will automatically tilt, looking like the bottom left corner is the center point.

4. What was the most common action in your two minute interaction with the experience?

I'm playing with the line that follows the cursor and activating the big circle. I'm also moving back and forth on the website to see the transitions between each section and focus more on how it is displayed. But the most common one is changing colors between the projects' background.

5. What is your impression of the intended primary goal of the interactive experience?

It's very creative, and each result of the work is structured in each section, which makes it accessible. However, it's hard to figure out how to go back to the gallery page if I click on one of them. I love the color choices and the fonts. The transitions are very flowy and smooth when changing each section.

6. How does the interactive experience communicate this primary goal?

It communicates through a simple design with a structured layout. The buttons for contact information, biography, and works are easy to navigate. The interactive links allows users to engage deeper with Brown's work by understanding his expertise and allowing people to explore his works without searching from external page.
